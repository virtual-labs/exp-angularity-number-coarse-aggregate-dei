#### Step 8: Procedure for Conducting Sieve Analysis

• Click on lid to cover the sieve stack.<br><img src="procedure_images/8.1.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
 
• Click on the sieve stack to place it on shaker.<br><img src="procedure_images/8.2.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
 
• Click on the green button to start the machine.<br><img src="procedure_images/8.3.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
 
• Click on the lid to remove the sieve from the shaker.<br><img src="procedure_images/8.4.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
 
• Click on the sieve to open it and record aggregates from each sieve.<br><img src="procedure_images/8.5.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
 
• Click NEXT to proceed <br> 

#### Step 9: Aggregate Sample Preparation Procedure
•	Click on tray to fill aggregate in cylinder in three layers.<br><img src="procedure_images/9.1.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
 
•	compact each layer with 100 blows using tamping rod by clicking on it and remove excess of the material .<br><img src="procedure_images/9.2.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
  
•	Repeat this process three times by clicking on the aggregates tray and rod respectively .<br>

• Click NEXT to proceed <br>
